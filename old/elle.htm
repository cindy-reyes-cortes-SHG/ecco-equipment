ECCO in the News: ELLE Magazine

A few years back, southern California fashion producer, Peter McClafferty, was driving by a construction site in Palos Verdes and saw some of our rental equipment. He thought it would make a good background for a fashion spread so he called the number on the decal. Once he learned about the additional equipment we had in the Santa Ana yard, however, he quickly determined that ECCO should be the location for the shoot. A few weeks later a group of fashion professionals descended upon the yard for two days to shoot a photo spread for ELLE magazine.

It was a fun diversion for the workers in Santa Ana. Joe Saragosa was especially helpful in maneuvering the equipment. The ELLE crew brought a luxury RV on site to handle wardrobe changes, makeup and hair. The amount of time actually spent taking pictures was surprisingly little. Most of the time was used to get everything just right. The results were an 18 page center photo spread in the August 2008 issue of ELLE Italy.
